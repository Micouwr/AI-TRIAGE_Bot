# AI Triage Bot Governance

This directory contains the governance documents for the AI Triage Bot Prototype, outlining the configuration, roles, responsibilities, and operational guidelines.

## Contents

- **config/**: Houses the `scope.yaml` file which defines the system scope, intended use, and details about the AI Triage Bot Prototype.
- **roles_and_responsibilities.md**: Specifies the roles and responsibilities of the individuals and teams involved in the governance and operation of the AI system.

## Purpose

The documents within this directory are essential for ensuring that the AI Triage Bot Prototype operates ethically, transparently, and in compliance with relevant standards and regulations.

## Usage

To understand the governance framework of the AI Triage Bot Prototype, review the documents in this directory. They provide a comprehensive guide to the system's configuration and the roles involved in its governance.

## Review Schedule

- **Monthly**: Review and update the `scope.yaml` and `roles_and_responsibilities.md` as needed.
- **Quarterly**: Conduct a governance review to ensure ongoing compliance with ISO/IEC 42001 standards.

## Reference Documents

- `config/scope.yaml`: System configuration and intended use.
- `roles_and_responsibilities.md`: Definitions of governance roles and responsibilities.
